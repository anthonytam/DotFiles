Assignment two extension

By popular demand, there is an extension for assignment two, to the end of Sunday February 28.

But I think that some students will have organized their time so as to complete the assignment by the original deadline (after all, all of the schedules were published in the first week of January), and I want to recognize that.

So there will be a 5% bonus for submitting the assignment by the original due time, decreasing continuously at the rate of 2% per day until it runs out at noon on Saturday (although the extension is still in force for another 36 hours). No assignments will be accepted after midnight at the end of Sunday.

The bonus will be computed for each file independently. I strongly recommend that you submit what you have for all files by the original deadline; then you can decide later in the week whether you want to submit something new to replace it. As always, the last-submitted file counts, both in the time of the file and the content of the file.

No late assignments will be accepted (without a medical or similar reason).
