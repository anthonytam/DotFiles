* States of the circuit
The circuit has the following states:
- 000 (Initial)
- 001 (1 Click - To arm)
- 011 (2 Clicks - To arm)
- 010 (3 Clicks - Armed)
- 110 (1 Click - To disarm)
- 100 (2 Clicks - To disarm)
- 101 (3 Clicks - Disarmed)
